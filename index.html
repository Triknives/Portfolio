About Me, And How I Got Here

For the past 10 years, I have been working in the Mental Health sector in the Portland, Oregon and Vancouver, Washington area. I initially started in field as a Lifeline Volunteer Operator, which is a suicide prevention support service located in the Portland area which provided support to calls across the nation. Upon finishing my Masters in Marriage, Couple and Family Therapy I worked as a Child and Family Therapist. While also allowing me to continue engaging in activities I enjoy and being a part of a new community.
